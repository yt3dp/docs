High heat will start to melt your prints, and make them feel sticky. If they feel stickier after drying than before, cool them down with cold air before curing.</p></div><div class="sd-card" target="_self"><h2>Air drying</h2><p>Pat away most of the moisture with paper towels or a cloth.</p><p>If washed with IPA, leave for a few hours.
